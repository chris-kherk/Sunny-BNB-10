Property Onboarding With Sunny BNB

Sunny BNB

This article is designed to walk you through the onboarding process for new properties with Sunny BNB, catering to short-term rentals. The process, while generally consistent, may vary slightly depending on each property owner's unique circumstances. Here, we outline the 12-step procedure from the initial contact right through to the listing of your property, ensuring you know what to expect when you decide to join Sunny BNB.

Initial Contact

  • The process begins with a conversation between Sunny BNB and the property owner to discuss the specifics of the property, including its location, layout, and the owner’s needs. This is followed by a follow-up email detailing information about Sunny BNB and the services we provide.

Inspection And Appraisal

  • A thorough inspection of the property is essential to assess its suitability for short-term rentals (STR). During the visit, we evaluate the property’s features, advise on any necessary cleaning or maintenance, suggest the best configuration for beds, and determine the optimal number of guests. Following this, owners receive an Information Pack detailing Sunny BNB's services.

Agreement On Management

  • If both parties agree to proceed after the appraisal, we move forward with formalizing the management agreement.

Property Listing Information

  • The property owner is required to fill out a detailed form with all particulars of the property. This information is crucial for creating accurate listings on platforms like Airbnb, Booking.com, and Stayz.

Form 6 Agreement

  • In compliance with Queensland state legislation, a Form 6 agreement is prepared. This formal agreement between Sunny BNB (as the registered agent) and the property owner includes an annexure detailing specific management terms.

Preparation Of Property

  • The property owner is responsible for furnishing the property and preparing it for our cleaners, who will ensure everything meets our high standards, including making beds and providing towels.

Professional Photos

  • A professional photographer takes between 24 to 35 photos, including 3 drone shots. High-quality photos are crucial as they serve as the property’s public showcase.

Listing Of Property

  • We begin by creating a profile on Airbnb to take advantage of the 14-day priority window given to new listings. Subsequently, we list the property on Airbnb, Booking.com, Stayz, and our own site, sunnycoastbnb.com.au. Our pricing software updates all listings and pricing twice daily.

Guest Bookings

  • We handle guest bookings, messaging, and identity verification, and provide guest reviews and feedback on the relevant booking platforms.

Cleaning Of Property

  • Our cleaners prepare the property for new guests using only high-quality linen service items. Property owners are asked to provide doona covers and mattress protectors.

Post Guest Check Out

  • We assess the property for any damages or rubbish, bill the guest if necessary, and manage claims for damages and other financial matters.

Owner Payments

  • Property owners have access to all booking details through an online portal, where they can also make personal bookings. Payments to owners are made mid-month and at the end of each month.

This structured approach ensures that each property is managed with the utmost professionalism and care, from the initial setup to the ongoing management and guest interactions. With Sunny BNB, property owners can rest assured that their properties are in good hands, maximizing their returns and ensuring guest satisfaction.
